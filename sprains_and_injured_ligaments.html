Sprains and Injured Ligaments

Sprains are injuries to ligaments. If there is a popping sound or sensation, the injury may be more serious, such as a broken bone. Symptoms of sprains include swelling, tenderness, and difficulty moving the affected area. All but the most minor sprains should be evaluated by a medical professional.
