Diabetes

Diabetes comes in many forms; type 1, type 2, and gestational. The metabolic disease can be caused by poor diet, obesity, medications, genetic mutations, or even damage to the pancreas. Common symptoms range from increased hunger and/or thirst, extreme fatigue, blurry vision, slow healing of cuts and bruises, weight loss, to tingling in the hands/feet. Call a doctor immediately if you are experiencing any of the symptoms listed above.
