Chronic Conditions

Chronic conditions are illnesses that are persistent and long-lasting. Chronic conditions can last anywhere between three months and a lifetime.

Chronic conditions can be visible, physical conditions (i.e. paralysis) or may be unnoticeable “invisible illnesses” (i.e. Multiple Sclerosis). This umbrella term covers many different types of illnesses with varying symptoms and severities. All chronic conditions should be treated to maximize happiness and quality of life.
