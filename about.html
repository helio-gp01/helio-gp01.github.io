About this App

The purpose of this app is to help USF students locate basic Health Center services, resources, and health information.



This app was meant to be used on mobile devices in the vertical, "upright" orientation. Please use your browser's back button when navigating this app.



This app was intended for use by USF Tampa campus students. It will be updated when implementations for USF St. Pete and USF Sarasota-Bradenton are complete.

To change the language of the page, tap or click the "G" (Google) Language button, and select your desired language. Only machine translation is currently supported, but thorough localizations are planned.
