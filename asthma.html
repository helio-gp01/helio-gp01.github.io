Asthma

Asthma is a chronic lung condition in which a person's airways become inflamed and swollen, which results in difficulty breathing. A factor contributing to this difficulty breathing is the extra production of mucus that blocks the airways.

Common symptoms of asthma include: random coughing fits, coughing fits during exercise, difficulty breathing, frequent respiratory infections, and chest tightness.
