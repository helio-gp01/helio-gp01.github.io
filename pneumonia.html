Pneumonia

Pneumonia is a more serious respiratory infection. Common symptoms include perpetual coughing, fever, chills, shortness of breath, a sharp or stacking pain in the chest, headache, and fatigue. It is contagious and must be treated by a physician who can prescribe drugs to treat the illness.
