Emergency Health Sevices

For medical emergencies, please dial 911. While USF maintains a health clinic, these facilities may not be adequate to handle immediate life threatening situations. Again, if you are in need of urgent medical services, please dial 911. Several state of the art medical facilities are available near the university campus and are equipped to assist with any emergency.
