Neurological Disorders

Any biochemical, electrical or structural abnormality in the brain, spinal cord or nerves that can result in various symptoms (such as brain tumours, epilepsy, non-epileptic seizures, strokes and Parkinson's disease). Common physical symptoms include partial or complete paralysis, muscle weakness, loss of sensation, difficulty reading/writing, seizures, poor cognition, unexplained pain, and decreased alertness.

If you feel you are suffering from a neurological disorder please seek a medical referral for a consultation with a local neurologist. USF Bulls Country Pharmacy USF also offers prescription refills for those currently taking medication. Those suffering from chronic neurological disorders and/or seizures should seek immediate medical attention.
