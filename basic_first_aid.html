Substance Abuse

Binge drinking, chronic drinking and other substance abuse issues are problems on college campuses and in society in general. If you feel, or if your friends and loved ones have mentioned you may partake too much, or if you often have problems after or while you drink, then it could be time to seek some help. Here are some links that may help with providing answers for you.

Substances include but are not limited to drugs, alcohol and tobacco. Excessive substance use can interfere with your quality of life in many ways. If you are concerned that substances may be interfering with your life or someone you know, refer to these resources for information on the steps to improvement.
