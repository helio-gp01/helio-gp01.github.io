Common Illnesses

Illnesses include viral and bacterial infections that may go away on their own or require treatment when symptoms are severe. Some illnesses are prevented by vaccination. Some vaccinations are required for USF students, others are optional, but encouraged.

Possible symptoms include sore throat, vomiting, and coughing.Tight living spaces in college dorms contributes to illnesses spread.
